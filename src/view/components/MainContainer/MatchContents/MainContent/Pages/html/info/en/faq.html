How To…
Register
Click register button on top right of the web page. Enter all details about you following 3-step wizard. On the last step click Register My Account button. You will receive an email with activation hyperlink in your main box. Follow this link to finish your registration process. After that you can login and play.
Deposit
You need to deposit some funds in order to play with Koolbet237.com. Go to My Account, My Wallet and click Deposit. Select the deposit method. At the moment we only work with MTN. User your MTN account or credit card to execute payment through them. Upon successful deposit you can play immediately.
BET
Select desired games outcomes, which you believe will win. All selected outcomes appear on your betslip. Select bet type – SINGLE, MULTIPLE, or SYSTEM. Enter stake. Click Place Bet.
Check Your Balance
Upon completion of registration process, the gaming account is created for you. Your balance is the amount of funds, which are stored on your gaming account. You can always see amount of your balance, if you log in to Koolbet237.com - on top right of the web page. To further see details of your balance click My Account and My Wallet. If a bonus campaign is currently applicable for you, you can see how your total balance is split between real balance and bonus balance.
Book a Bet
Whenever you have some Outcomes in your Betslip, you can Save this Betslip. By doing this you get a booking code, which you can later use to place bet on Koolbet237.com website or in the authorized betting shop. You can get a booking code to your email or share it by sending to your friends by email.
Withdraw
Whenever you with to withdraw some or all funds from your gaming account, go to My Account and My Wallet. There you can click Withdraw and select withdraw method. Currently we work with MTN as the payment gateway. Enter desired amount and complete the process by passing authorization process with MTN.
Change Your Password
It is recommended to change your password periodically to secure your privacy. Go to My Account, Privacy. Locate and click Change password link on top of page. Enter your current password and new password and Save.
