FUTSAL
All match markets are based on the result at the end of a scheduled 40 minutes play unless otherwise stated. This includes any added injury or stoppage time but does not include extra-time, time allocated for a penalty shootout or golden goal.
Match Abandonments/Postponements
• If a match is abandoned at any time all bets will be void • An unplayed or postponed match will be treated as void unless it is played within 48 hours of the listed kick-off time
Main Markets
Match Result (3-way) Predict the result of the match. Totals (2-way) Predict the total number of goals scored in the match.
