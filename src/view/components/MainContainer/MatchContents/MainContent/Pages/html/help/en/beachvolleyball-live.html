IMPORTANT
• In the case of a match not being finished, all undecided markets are considered void. • Golden set is not considered in any of the mentioned markets.
SETTLEMENT AND CANCELLATION RULES
• If a match is interrupted or postponed and is not continued within 48h after initial kick-off date, betting will be void. • If markets remain open with an incorrect score which has a significant impact on the prices, we reserve the right to void betting. • If a team retires all undecided markets are considered void.
